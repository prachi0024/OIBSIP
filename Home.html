DARLING Or DEVIL ?

A girl with a fighter mind

Author: Prachi Manish Patil

In every chapter of this book, you will get to see darling or the devil

And frequently people ask Gauri what have you achieved?

Life is all about struggle.

In this book that girl continuously strives for happiness in her life.

Happiness is the real motive for which she struggles in her life.

But for that she has to fight, she has to endure the pain, the hardships.

not only that she has to struggle with herself also because if she can conquer herself,

then she can be victorious in any front of her life.....

As She always said that:-

"Mere har samasya ka karan bhi mai,

Mere har samasya ka nivaran bhi mai"

She gave the key to unlocking the treasure of happiness which she seeks for her whole lives.
